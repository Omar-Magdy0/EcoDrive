9/*
10
11 AEBFV0 (Asynchronous Expandable Binary frame version 0)
12
13 Frame format:
14 [0x00 SYNC_BYTE][1Byte Payload Size][1Byte MCOBS_CODE][MCOBS_ENCODED([6Bit DeviceID][10Bit ServiceID][Payload][CRC16])]
15
16 CRC if for entire frame including all bytes except the SYNC_BYTE
17 total Frame encoding overhead
18 1(0x00) + 1(Payload Size) + 2(DeviceID+ServiceID) + 1(MCOBS firstCodeByte) + 2(CRC16) = 7
19*/
